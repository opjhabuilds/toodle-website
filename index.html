How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No complex onboarding. Open the app, get going, win your day.

1

Dump it all in

Add every task in seconds from the home screen quick-add bar. Set a theme, priority, dates, and recurrence. Get the mental clutter out. Toodle holds it all.

2

Toodle organises it

Tasks auto-sort by priority. P0 items surface with alerts. Smart filters show exactly what matters today. Assign tasks to family, friends, or colleagues with one tap.

3

Swipe, earn stars, repeat

Swipe right to complete. Watch your progress bar fill. Earn 5 stars. End the day opening the Done tab as proof you got things done.
